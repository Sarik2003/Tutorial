About My Camu

MyCamu is school-home collaboration portal, powered by Camu. Your Campus.
MyCamu provides students and parents all the information they want in one place, accessible from mobile app or on a web browser.
To use "MyCamu", your school must be running the Camu to manage the campus. Please get in touch with your school for more information.
